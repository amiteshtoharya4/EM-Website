Entrerepreneurs Meet
2018

Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology, in association with Evento Opus(a student initiated event management organisation) and with the help of SMUTBI(a startup incubator at SMIT) proudly presents Entrerepreneurs Meet'18, a start-up conference in NorthEast India.

The conference aims at giving a platform to budding ideas and a platform to entrepreneurs to implement their dreams. Besides being instruemental in bringing together new ideas, Entrerepreneurs Meet also gives the best chance to discover the lengths and breadths of the business world.

MUMBAI
DABBAWALAS

The Mumbai Dabbawalas were represented by Dr. Pawan Agarwal.
Mumbai Dabbawalas union got formed in the year 1890. Since then the union has had an estimated growth of 5-10%. They are known to be the finest in Supply Chain Management . Their unique system of color coding and simple operation have fetched them recognition worldwide,be it a certification from ISO and Six Sigma for 99.9% efficient deliveries (a feat they achieved without the use of an application), world record in best time management, an entry in the Guinness Book of World Records or an honourable mention at Ripley's - believe it or not.

CURATED
PITCHING

The Curated Pitching Competition is where budding entrepreneurs got a big opportunity of getting their projects funded and kickstart their projects in their respective startup. They pitched their ideas to investors and mentors of the industry who gave them valuable advice regarding their projects.
